Images from the same deck must be in the same block, i.e. no empty line between them.

```markdown
> [!cards-deck]+ Clue
> ## Who
> 
> ![[image1.png]]
> ![[image2.png]]
> ![[image3.png]]
> 
> ## How
> 
> ![[image4.png]]
> ![[image5.png]]
> ![[image6.png]]
>
> ## Where
> 
> ![[image7.png]]
> ![[image8.png]]
> ![[image9.png]]
```
